Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Tower Troll (also listed as Tower Troll: Trap Climb) is a mobile game developed by Genaxa ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Tower Troll does not require account creation, login, or registration. We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number. However, third-party services we use (AdMob, Firebase) may collect certain device and usage data as described below.

Locally Stored Data

Game progress (scores, coins, settings, unlocked content) is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences and a local Room database. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. Google may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's Google settings.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment information. All billing is handled by Google as described in Google Payments Privacy Notice.

Children's Privacy

Tower Troll is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how players interact with the game (e.g., which floors are played most, game modes used) and Firebase Crashlytics to detect and fix crashes. These services collect device information, app usage data, and crash logs. No personally identifiable information is collected. See Firebase Privacy.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Google Play Billing — in-app purchases
  • Firebase Analytics — usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top.
